Description

This is a recipe to make a quick and easy homemade pizza. It is a simple ingredient list making it amazing for beginners to follow along and make some pizza. The list consist of 6 ingredients that include water, package active dry yeast, white sugar, bread flour, olive oil, salt. Making this pizza will take about 45 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 Â½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly coat the pizza pan with grease.
  2. Get a bowl and pour warm water. Add yeast and sugar into the bowl. Then mix and let stand until creamy for about 10 minutes.
  3. To the bowl add flour, oil, and salt to the mixture. Either by hand or a stand mixer with a dough hook beat the dough until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Flatten out the dough onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Place the dough onto a pizza pan.
  7. Spread sauce and toppings of choice.
  8. Bake in the oven until golden brown. 15 to 20 minutes then remove from oven and let it cool for 5 minutes before serving.
